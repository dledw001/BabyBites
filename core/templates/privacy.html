Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BabyBites collects, uses, and protects information about you and your child when you use our service.

1. Information we collect

We collect account information (such as your email address) and baby profile data you enter into the app (such as name, date of birth, and feeding information).

2. How we use your information

We use this information to provide core BabyBites features, such as tracking feedings, generating daily reports, and displaying insights in your dashboard.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share information with service providers as needed to operate the application (for example, hosting and infrastructure).

4. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.
