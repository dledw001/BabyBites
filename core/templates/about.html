About BabyBites

BabyBites is a simple, focused app that helps parents track what their baby eats, when they eat, and how they react to new foods.

Why we built it

Introducing solids can be overwhelming. BabyBites gives you a single place to log feedings, reactions, and milestones so you don’t have to juggle notes, spreadsheets, or half-remembered details.

What BabyBites does
  • Track daily feedings by food, portion size, and reaction.
  • Generate simple daily reports you can review or share.
  • Highlight “first time” foods and other basic milestones.
What BabyBites is not

BabyBites is not a medical app and does not replace your pediatrician. It’s a record-keeping tool meant to support your own judgment and conversations with healthcare professionals.
